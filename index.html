About Us

AI-Driven Plant Disease Detection

PhytoSense offers cutting-edge AI and machine learning technology to quickly and accurately detect plant diseases, ensuring healthier crops and greener gardens.

PhytoSense revolutionizes agriculture by harnessing the power of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) to detect diseases in plants swiftly with upto 96% acccuracy . By scanning the plant's leaves, this innovative system analyzes intricate patterns and subtle anomalies indicative of diseases. This technology enables farmers to take proactive measures to mitigate crop damage and ensure optimal yield.
